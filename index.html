After Divorce Mediation: What to Expect

After divorce mediation, what to expect can seem daunting. This article will help you understand what the process entails and what it means for your future. Divorce mediation is an option that many couples choose when they are not able to come to a mutually agreed upon decision about how their assets should be divided or if there are any emotional ties involved in custody agreements. It’s important to note that this type of divorce is different from what most people think of as “mediation” where parties sit down with a mediator who helps them reach decisions together without lawyers negotiating on their behalf. In this case, lawyers negotiate on the couple’s behalf after they have already determined what each party wants by going through mediation sessions first.
